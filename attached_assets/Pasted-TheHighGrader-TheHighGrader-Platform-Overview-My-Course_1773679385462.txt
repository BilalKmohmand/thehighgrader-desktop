Student Instructions
In this assignment, you will work on a problem set focused on fractions. You are tasked with solving a series of problems that test your understanding of fraction operations, comparisons, and applications. The problem set consists of ten individual questions, each designed to challenge your mastery of the topic. Please ensure you show all your work in detail to demonstrate your thought process and problem-solving techniques. 

Start by reviewing the key concepts related to fractions, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and converting between mixed numbers and improper fractions. After completing your review, work through each question in the problem set, clearly labeling your answers. Remember that neatness and clarity are important; present your work in a logical and organized manner, using proper mathematical notation. 

You will submit your completed problem set as a single document. This document should be in PDF format to ensure that your formatting remains consistent. If you have completed the assignment by hand, please scan or take clear pictures of your work to compile into one PDF. Ensure all pages are legible, with answers neatly displayed. Your submission is due by [insert due date here]. Late submissions will face a grade penalty unless prior arrangements have been made. 

As a reminder, the work you submit should be your own. Collaboration is encouraged for learning purposes, but each student must present their individual work. Academic integrity is paramount in this assignment, so please be mindful of this as you complete your problems.
